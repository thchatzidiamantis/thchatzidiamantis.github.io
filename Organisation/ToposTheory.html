UWO Reading Seminar on Topos Theory, Fall 2025

Organisers: Theofanis Chatzidiamantis-Christoforidis and Thomas Thorbjørnsen

Meetings: Mondays, 13:30-15:30, room MC108.

Schedule

  • [15.09.2025] Talk 1: Introduction. Speaker: Theofanis Chatzidiamantis-Christoforidis.
  • [22.09.2025] Talk 2: Heyting Algebras and Logic in a Topos. Speaker: Zack Dooley.
  • [29.09.2025] Talk 3: Grothendieck Topoi, part I. Speakers: Alex Zwart and Mac Martin.
  • [06.10.2025] Talk 4: Grothendieck Topoi, part II. Speakers: Alex Zwart and Mac Martin.
  • [13.10.2025] Talk 5: Lawvere-Tierney Topologies. Speaker: Elias Vandenberg.
  • [20.10.2025] Talk 6: Colimits and Slices of Topoi. Speaker: Adrien Grenier.
  • [27.10.2025] Talk 7: Set-theoretic Forcing.
  • [03.11.2025] Talk 8: Forcing in a Topos.
